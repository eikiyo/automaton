# Guidance: Measurement & Variables (D09)
## Gates: D09-01 through D09-09 | Critical: D09-01

### What JIBS Demands
- D09-01 (CRITICAL): Operationalization of each construct specified in detail.
- D09-02: Reliability coefficients reported for survey/scale items.
- D09-03: Construct validity assessed (convergent and discriminant).
- D09-04: Measurement equivalence tested for cross-national surveys.
- D09-05: Proxy variables justified when direct measures unavailable.
- D09-06: Direction of coding unambiguous.
- D09-07: Control variables theoretically justified, not kitchen-sink.
- D09-08: Measurement error acknowledged and addressed.
- D09-09: Country-level moderators matched to right theoretical dimension.

### How to Pass

**For Review/Conceptual Papers:**
Focus on D09-01 and D09-07. When discussing how prior studies measured constructs:
- State how each key variable was operationalized across studies.
- Note measurement inconsistencies across the literature.
- Propose construct definitions that resolve measurement ambiguity.

**Control Variable Justification (D09-07):**
Every control must have a theoretical reason. Never list controls as "commonly used in prior research" without explaining WHY they matter for YOUR specific model.
