# Guidance: Literature Positioning (D03)
## Gates: D03-01 through D03-08 | Critical: D03-01

### What JIBS Demands
- D03-01 (CRITICAL): Gap is specific — a precise void, tension, or paradox — not "little research has examined X."
- D03-02: Literature review synthesizes, not lists — reveals structure, conflict, or evolution.
- D03-03: Positioned against at least two streams of prior work.
- D03-04: Seminal and canonical IB works cited.
- D03-05: Literature from past 5 years included.
- D03-06: Conflicting findings acknowledged, not cherry-picked.
- D03-07: Contribution stated relative to what it overturns, extends, or reconciles.
- D03-08: Literature review proportionate — focused on the gap, not encyclopedic.

### How to Pass (from ARA Writer & Synthesis phases)

**Literature Review Structure (D03-02, D03-03):**
Organize by STREAMS from Synthesis, not by paper. Each stream becomes a subsection.

For each stream:
1. Opening sentence: What this body of work collectively argues.
2. Key findings with citations (argument-driven, NOT paper-driven).
3. Methodological patterns: How has this been studied?
4. Limitations within the stream: What hasn't this stream addressed?
5. Bridge to next stream: How does this connect?

After all streams, include a SYNTHESIS paragraph:
"Taken together, these streams reveal [the gap]. While Stream 1 establishes [X] and Stream 2 demonstrates [Y], no existing work has [Z]. This paper addresses this gap by..."

**ARGUMENT-DRIVEN WRITING RULE (D03-02):**
NEVER write: "Smith (2021) found X. Jones (2022) found Y. Lee (2023) argued Z."
ALWAYS write: "[Argument point], as evidenced by [finding] (Smith, 2021; Jones, 2022), though [qualification] (Lee, 2023)."
The subject of every sentence should be the CONCEPT, MECHANISM, or ARGUMENT — never the author.

**Gap Specificity (D03-01):**
BAD: "Little research has examined fintech in emerging markets."
GOOD: "Despite extensive research on fintech adoption (Stream 1) and institutional voids (Stream 2), no study has examined how institutional voids specifically shape the reverse innovation pathway through which emerging-market fintech solutions are transferred to developed markets."

**Seminal Works (D03-04):**
For IB topics, cite at minimum:
- Dunning (OLI), Buckley & Casson (internalization), Johanson & Vahlne (Uppsala), Peng (institution-based view), Kostova (institutional distance), Govindarajan (reverse innovation — if relevant).
- Check which seminal works are relevant to YOUR specific topic.

**Recency (D03-05):**
At least 40% of citations should be from the last 5 years. If below this, the review looks outdated.

**Conflict Acknowledgment (D03-06):**
Use the Contradiction Map. For each tension:
- Present both sides with citations.
- Explain possible moderators or methodological differences.
- Use GRADE-calibrated language for evidence strength.
