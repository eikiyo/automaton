# Guidance: Robustness & Validity (D13)
## Gates: D13-01 through D13-07 | No critical gates

### What JIBS Demands
- D13-01: Robustness checks with alternative model specification.
- D13-02: Results robust to alternative operationalizations.
- D13-03: Common method bias assessed for survey data.
- D13-04: Influential observations and outliers assessed.
- D13-05: Subgroup heterogeneity explored.
- D13-06: Results hold when specific countries/regions excluded.
- D13-07: Sensitivity analysis for contestable assumptions.
