# Guidance: Verified Citations (D04)
## Gates: D04-01 through D04-07 | Critical: D04-01, D04-02

### What JIBS Demands
- D04-01 (CRITICAL): Every empirical claim attributed to a study must actually be reported by that study.
- D04-02 (CRITICAL): No cited paper's conclusion is reversed or overstated.
- D04-03: Direct quotes have page numbers.
- D04-04: All in-text citations present in reference list (no orphans).
- D04-05: All references cited in text (no ghosts).
- D04-06: Statistical figures from prior work traceable to original source.
- D04-07: No self-citation chains inflating support.

### How to Pass (from ARA Writer phase)

**ZERO PHANTOM CITATIONS RULE:**
Every (Author, Year) citation MUST exist in your paper database.
Before writing each section:
1. Use available papers to get the list.
2. ONLY cite papers that appear in the database.
3. If you need a citation for a claim and can't find a matching paper, OMIT the claim rather than invent a citation.

**CLAIM GROUNDING RULE:**
Every factual claim must trace to either:
- A specific extracted claim from the database (preferred).
- A paper's abstract/metadata (acceptable for background claims).
- Common knowledge in the field (no citation needed, but use sparingly).

Never assert findings without grounding. If evidence is mixed, write about mixed evidence — don't resolve it artificially.

**Citation Density Requirements:**
- Introduction: 8+ citations.
- Literature Review: 20+ citations.
- Methods: 5+ citations.
- Results: 10+ citations.
- Discussion: 8+ citations.
- Conclusion: 3+ citations.
- TOTAL: 35+ unique citations.

**Citation Integrity Rules:**
- Use (Author, Year) format consistently.
- When multiple papers support a claim, cite all: (Author1, Year; Author2, Year).
- Aim for 1 citation per 2-3 sentences in lit review, 1 per 3-4 sentences elsewhere.

**Journal Tier Priority:**
- For every 1 unranked citation, include 2 from top-tier sources (FT50, UTD24, ABS 4*/4).
- At least 40% of citations from top-tier journals.
- When two papers support the same claim, PREFER the higher-tier journal.
- In lit review and theory sections, lead with top-tier sources.

**Self-Validation:**
After writing, check:
- Every (Author, Year) in text exists in References.
- Every entry in References is cited in text.
- No single paper cited more than 8 times (concentration risk).
- Citations spread across streams, not clustered around 3-4 papers.
